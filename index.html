About Me

I'm Eatheswar N

ASIC Design Engineer · VLSI Student · Open Source Creator

I am a 2nd year B.E. Electronics Engineering student specialising in VLSI Design & Technology at R.M.K College of Engineering, Chennai. I explore the complete chip design pipeline — from writing synthesisable RTL in Verilog all the way to GDS II tapeout.

I built Silis — an open-source EDA platform so that every student can access the full RTL-to-GDS II flow without paying for expensive tools. Silis is now adopted across multiple engineering colleges. I prefer to keep learning, continue challenging myself, and build things that actually matter.
